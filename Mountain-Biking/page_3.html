The Bunnyhop:

  • With the Bunnyhop you can jump over every obstacle in the city and in the forest
  • The Bunnyhop is essential for big kickers

The Stoppie:

  • The stoppie is important for really tight curves, so you can do a 90Â° turn on your front wheel.
  • By doing this you don't have to step down from your bike.
  • Video-Tutorial

Wheelie/Manual:

  • While doing a Wheelie or a Manual you are riding on your back-wheel only.
  • When performing a Wheelie, you are still cycling.
  • However, when performing the manual, you don't cycle, but rather balance your weight on one wheel and drive with the momentum you have.
  • The Wheelie and the Manual are basic skills for the Bunnyhop and for jumps.
