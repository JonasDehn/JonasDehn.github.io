What to know about MOUNTAIN-BIKING

At first, you should buy a bike

There a multiple options to choose:

  • Trailbike: is 50% uphill and 50% downhill
  • All mountain bike: is 40% uphill and 60% downhill
  • Enduro bike: is 30% uphill and 70% downhill
  • Downhill bike: 10% uphill and 90% downhill
