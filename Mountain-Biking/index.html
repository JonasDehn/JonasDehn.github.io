What to know about MOUNTAIN-BIKING

At first, you should buy a bike

Trailbike:

  • You can drive 50% uphill and 50% downhill
  • Up to 130mm of travel in the fork and full suspension. You can buy it without full suspension too.
  • Most people start biking with the Trail or All mountain bike
  • Most bikes have 29 inch tires

All mountain bike:

  • You can drive 40% uphill and 60% downhill
  • Between 140 and 150 mm travel in the fork and the fully
  • An All-Mountain Bike is an excellent option to start biking
  • The All-Mountain Bike can be bought with 27,5 inches tires too.

Enduro bike:

  • You can drive 30% uphill and 70% downhill
  • Travel between 170 and 180 mm
  • The Enduro is the perfect option to drive in the bikepark but also uphill.
  • Tires: 27,5 and 29 inches

Downhill bike:

  • You can drive 10% uphill and 90% downhill
  • Travel between 200 and 220 mm
  • The downhill bike is only used in the bikepark, because it is to heavy.
  • Tires: only 27,5 inches

E-MTB:

  • The E-MTB is a bike which has electrical support
  • Travel between 150 and 200 mm
  • The E-MTB is the perfect option to drive comfortable with less affort uphill but you can ride it on big trails too.
  • Tires: 27,5 and 29 inches
