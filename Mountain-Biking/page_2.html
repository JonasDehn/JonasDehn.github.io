What you should know before buying a Mountain Bike

There are multiple things to consider:

Get the right size. First up, everything else is secondary to the right frame size: You can choose between 4 different sizes: S,M,L,XL,XXL

The next important step is to choose the right Wheel size: 26",27,5",29"

Don’t obsess about weight: Check the reviews of the forks and shocks (on full suspension) on the bike you’re looking at, and use the manufacturer’s site to get the exact models!

Choose suspension quality, not quantity: Weight is important, but off-road, strength is marginally more important!

Keep some budget back: Often you need some parts to replace the broken ones or bald tyres. Additionally, there are a lot of necessary parts you need like a Fidlock bottle or a Multi-Tool
