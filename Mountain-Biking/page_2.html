What you should know before buying a Mountain Bike

  • Get the right size. First up, everything else is secondary to the right frame size: You can choose between 4 different sizes: S,M,L,XL,XXL
  • The next important step is to choose the right Wheel size: 26",27,5",29"
  • Don’t obsess about weight: Check the reviews of the forks and shocks (on full susspension) on the bike you’re looking at, and use the manufacturer’s site to get the exact models!
  • Choose suspension quality, not quantity: Weight is important, but off-road, strength is marginally more important!
  • Keep some budget back: Often you need some parts to replace the broken ones or bald tyres. Moreover, there are a lot of necessary part you need like a fidlock bottle
